Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Labib ("we", "us", "our") provides a CRM assistant designed to help teams manage leads and automate follow-ups. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, services, and integrations.

Information We Collect

Personal information: name, email address, company name, and contact details you provide when you sign up or request a demo.

CRM Data: when you connect Labib to your CRM, we may access contacts, deals, notes, and related metadata to provide features (summaries, suggestions, automations).

Usage data: logs, device and browser information, feature usage to improve and secure the service.

How We Use Information

We use data to deliver and improve the service, personalize suggestions, send updates and billing information, respond to support requests, and for security and fraud prevention.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with: (a) service providers who process data on our behalf, (b) when required by law, and (c) with your consent. Integrations with third-party CRMs occur only after you authorize the connection.

Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect data in transit and at rest. However, no system is completely secure — if you detect a security issue, contact us immediately.

Retention

We retain personal data as needed to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting support.

Your Choices

You can manage notification preferences, disconnect CRM integrations, or request data export/deletion via account settings or by contacting us at privacy@labib.example.

Children

Our service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy. We will post the update date at the top of the page and, where required, notify you.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@labib.example.
